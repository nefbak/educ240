The Circles Of Change

About

Circles may seem simple, but their symbolism permeates nature, spirituality, and culture. This virtual art gallery explores how circles capture and convey key social mechanisms such as reproduction, authority, recognition, resistance, and liberty. Through a diverse range of art forms and images, the exhibit illustrates how circles symbolize unity, cycles, and continuity, whether seen in the orbits of celestial bodies or the ripples formed by a stone tossed into water. Each piece demonstrates the multifaceted ways circles manifest across our world, bridging traditional and contemporary expressions. This collection offers a personal interpretation of our learning journey, revealing how seemingly simple shapes encapsulate complex ideas about societal norms, individual freedoms, collective action, and cultural diversity.

The project aims to showcase how art and visual representation can deepen our understanding of complex concepts, reflecting insights gained from academic study. By presenting these interpretations in a digital format, the gallery engages viewers in a meaningful exploration of the profound symbolism and practical relevance of circles in our lives and educational systems. Initially focused on how circles are reproduced in various aspects of life, this project has expanded to encompass broader themes such as authority and liberation, creating a comprehensive and interconnected exploration through the symbolism of the everyday circle.
